Our Departments

Clubs

If you have experience with creating and running clubs, join our clubs department. Help guide students through the club making, running, and teaching process, and contribute to the growth of our national club network. Look to the contact page to email us with your qualifications and goals for Quest Andromeda.

Camps

If you have experience with teaching, astronomy, and physics, become an instructor for our camps. Create curricula with other instructors and teach in seasonal camps. Help make learning in astronomy accessible throughout the nation. Contact us now with your qualifications and goals!

Research

If you have experience with research in Astronomy, join our research department (UNDER DEVELOPMENT). Help guide students through the scientific method and the research process in astronomy. Help students understand the opportunities that lie within the future of astronomy. Contact us now with your qualifications and goals!

Astrophotography

If you are experienced with Astrophotography, join us in guiding students through their astrophotography journey (UNDER DEVELOPMENT). Guide students through budget and advanced astrophotography to help ALL students capture the night sky. Contact us now with your qualifications and goals!
